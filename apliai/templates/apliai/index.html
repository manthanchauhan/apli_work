About

Apli.ai is Indian born company founded at Global Entrepreneurship Boot camp at Indonesia which works towards helping Students to improve on their personality and to grab better opportunities with Artificial Intelligence at IIT Bombay.

Apli.ai was started at Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp at Jakarta, Indonesia We're a team of designers, engineers and computer scientists from IIT, IIM, NIT, IIIT and Mumbai University working towards helping students to improve upon their personality with AI.
